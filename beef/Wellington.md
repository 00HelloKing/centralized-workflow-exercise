Beef Wellington Recipe

Ingredients (Serves 4):
Beef tenderloin: 500g (center-cut, trimmed)
Puff pastry: 1 sheet (thawed if frozen)
Cremini or button mushrooms: 300g (finely chopped)
Prosciutto: 6-8 thin slices
Dijon mustard: 2 tbsp
Egg: 1 (for egg wash)
Garlic: 2 cloves (minced)
Fresh thyme: 1 tbsp (chopped)
Olive oil: 2 tbsp
Salt and black pepper: To taste
Optional: Foie gras pâté or truffle paste (for extra richness).

Step:
1. Sear the Beef
2. Prepare the Duxelles (Mushroom Mixture)
3. Assemble the Wellington
4. Wrap in Puff Pastry
