Classic Tiramisu recipe

Ingredients

·Finger biscuits (appropriate amount)
·Mascarpone cheese 250g
·Egg yolks 2
·Whipping cream 100g
·Granulated sugar 30g
·Espresso 100ml
·Coffee liqueur 10ml
·Gelatin sheets 5g
·Cocoa powder

Instructions

1.Prepare:
First, place the egg yolks and granulated sugar in a bowl and whisk them at high speed over a pot of hot water until the egg yolk and sugar solution doesn't immediately return to its original state when dropped.
Soften the mascarpone cheese at room temperature, then beat it at low speed until smooth. Pour in the whipping cream and continue beating.
Soften the gelatin sheets in cold water, then melt them over hot water. Add the melted gelatin to the cheese mixture, then add the whipped egg yolk mixture and stir. Subsequently, add the coffee liqueur and mix well.

2.Assemble Tiramisu:
Dip the finger biscuits in the espresso solution and lay them in a glass container.
Add the cheese and egg yolk paste and smooth it out with a silicone spatula.
Repeat the layers: dip another layer of finger biscuits in the espresso and lay them down, then add another layer of the cheese and egg yolk paste and smooth it out.
Finally, sift cocoa powder over the top.

3.Serve:
Place the tiramisu in the refrigerator and chill for about 3.5 hours.
Take it out and enjoy!
